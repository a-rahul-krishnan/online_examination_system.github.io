FAQ:


1. What is the Online Examination System?

The Online Examination System is a web-based platform designed to conduct assessments, tests, and examinations over the internet. It provides an efficient and user-friendly environment for educators, institutions, and organizations to create, administer, and manage online exams securely.

2. Is the Online Examination System secure?

Yes, security is our top priority. Our platform incorporates multiple layers of security measures, including data encryption, user authentication, and secure hosting. We implement advanced techniques to prevent cheating and ensure the authenticity of candidates' identities during exams.

3. Can the system accommodate both small-scale and large-scale assessments?

Yes, the Online Examination System is designed to be scalable. Whether you need to conduct a small classroom quiz or a nationwide examination with thousands of candidates, our platform can handle various scales of assessments without compromising on performance or user experience.

4. Can we integrate the Online Examination System with our existing Learning Management System?

Yes, we offer integration options to seamlessly connect our platform with your existing Learning Management System or other educational software. This integration streamlines data management and allows a more cohesive learning and assessment experience for students.

5. Can candidates take exams at their own convenience, or are there specific time slots?

It depends on the exam settings defined by the administrator. The Online Examination System supports both timed and untimed exams. Administrators can choose to set specific time slots for exams or allow candidates to take tests at their convenience within a defined time window.
